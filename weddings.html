Wedding Hair

Creating beautiful, lasting bridal looks for your special day

Your Perfect Wedding Hair

Your wedding day is one of the most important days of your life, and your hair should be nothing less than perfect. With over 21 years of experience, I specialise in creating stunning bridal styles that complement your dress, venue, and personal style.

Whether you dream of romantic loose curls, an elegant updo, or a beautifully braided style, I'll work with you to bring your vision to life and ensure your hair stays flawless throughout the day.

I can also style hair for your bridesmaids, mother of the bride, and other members of your wedding party, so everyone looks their best.

How It Works

A simple process to ensure you get exactly the look you want

Consultation

We'll discuss your wedding vision, dress, venue, and any ideas you have for your hair. Bring photos for inspiration.

Trial Session

A full trial run of your wedding hair, typically 4-6 weeks before the big day. We'll refine and perfect the look together.

Wedding Day

I'll arrive at your venue or home with plenty of time to style you and your wedding party, ensuring everyone is ready on time.

Touch-ups

Final adjustments and styling to ensure you look absolutely perfect as you walk down the aisle.

A trial session is highly recommended for all brides. This gives us time to experiment with different styles and ensure I create the perfect look for your big day. Trials are typically scheduled 4-6 weeks before the wedding.

Booking & Deposits

To secure your wedding date, a deposit is required at the time of booking. This ensures I'm reserved exclusively for you on your special day. The remaining balance is due before or on the wedding day. Please get in touch for current rates and availability.
